About LeftoverLink

We're on a mission to reduce food waste and build stronger communities through the simple act of sharing.

Our Mission

LeftoverLink was born from a simple idea: what if we could connect people who have extra food with those who need it? In a world where millions go hungry while tons of food goes to waste, we believe technology can bridge this gap.

Our platform makes it easy for individuals, restaurants, and organizations to share their surplus food with their community, reducing waste and helping those in need.
